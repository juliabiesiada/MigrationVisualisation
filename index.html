Who is the USA made of?

This visualisation provides information about the variability of country of birth and reason of entry of the migrants entering the Unites States of America over the years 2006-2019. The data can be filtered by name or year, and/or year. The first visualisation defaultly contains top 100 countries of birth in a given year (defaultly 2019), but this number can be modified using a slider. It is important to underline that not all of the data could be disclosed. For the purpose of this visualisation, the missing data was replaced with a 0 and the countries affected by it were marked with an asterisk (*).
